Space Racer by Marshall Wagner and Rodger Friesen!

Features added:
Collision detection added for Asteroids, Planets and Rings
Game displays game over message depending on if you win or lose
Draws hud in first person, has a free camera mode.
Asteroids spawn and move randomly
Spaceship accelerates and breaks, steering is handled by left mouse click and directing the camera.
Since camera directs movement, it can move in all direction.
Score and time are both displayed on the HUD, score is in upper left hand corner time is in lower right hand corner.


Features not implemented:
Playback and free view is a little wonky.

Special Features:
We have lighting implemented and the texturing from this morning is fixed. ^_^
